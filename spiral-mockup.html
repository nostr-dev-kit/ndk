Layout Pattern:
• Center avatar (1): 24px - highest z-index (on top)
• Spiral outward clockwise with increasing sizes
• Sizes: 24 → 30 → 38 → 48 → 60 → 76px
• Each avatar ~1.26x larger (Fibonacci ratio)
• Avatars touch/overlap for connected spiral
• Z-index decreases outward (1 always visible)
• Most important user at center, largest at end
